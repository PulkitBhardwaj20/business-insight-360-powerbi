## 🚀 Overview

Business Insight 360 is a multi-domain business intelligence solution designed to provide a unified view of company performance.
It enables stakeholders to track financial metrics, customer behavior, product performance, and operational efficiency in one place.

---

## 💡 Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to get a unified view of performance across departments, leading to fragmented and inefficient decision-making.

---

## ✅ Solution

This dashboard integrates data across Finance, Sales, Marketing, and Supply Chain to provide a centralized, interactive decision-making tool.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

* Executive-level overview dashboard
* Financial P&L analysis
* Customer performance tracking
* Product profitability analysis
* Market share trend analysis
* Forecast accuracy and risk insights
* Interactive filtering across region, product, customer, and time

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI – Data visualization
* Power Query – Data transformation (ETL)
* DAX – Business calculations and KPIs
* Snowflake Data Model – Scalable analytical structure

---

## 🧩 Data Model

* Fact Tables: Sales, Forecast, Costs
* Dimension Tables: Customer, Product, Market, Date
